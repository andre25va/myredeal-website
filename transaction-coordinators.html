Features

Everything you need to coordinate like a pro

From contract to close, MyReDeal keeps every task, deadline, and stakeholder organized in one powerful platform.

Smart Task Management

Auto-generated checklists for every transaction type. Never miss a deadline with intelligent due date tracking and reminders.

Stakeholder Coordination

Keep agents, buyers, sellers, lenders, and title companies all on the same page with shared portals and real-time updates.

Automated Communications

Send branded email and SMS updates automatically at each milestone. Stay compliant with built-in 10DLC messaging standards.

Document Management

Centralize all contracts, addenda, and disclosures. E-signature ready integrations with DocuSign and DotLoop built in.

Analytics & Reporting

Track pipeline performance, average close times, and team productivity. Make data-driven decisions to grow your business.

Compliance & Security

WCAG 2.1 accessible, SOC 2 compliant, and fully RESPA-aware. Your client data is protected at every step.

How It Works

From contract to close in 4 simple steps

MyReDeal guides you through every transaction with automated workflows that keep everything on track.

1

Create Your Transaction

Input property address, parties, and key dates. MyReDeal auto-populates your state-specific checklist instantly.

2

Invite All Parties

Agents, buyers, lenders, and title companies each get a tailored view of the transaction — only what they need to see.

3

Automate & Coordinate

Automated reminders, SMS updates, and document requests go out at the right time to the right people.

4

Close With Confidence

Hit the closing table with everything complete, documented, and filed. Your full audit trail is always accessible.
